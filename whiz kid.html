RULES

  • Whiz Kids as the name suggests is an event designed to test not only the intelligence and the knowledge of the 'kids' participating in the competition but also the sensory, visual and memory skills.
  • Each team must have 2 competitors.
  • There are three rounds.
  • The first round is the written qualifier.
  • The second round is the 'Brain Storm' round. The instructions of this round would be given on spot.
  • The final round is the vintage 'Buzzer Round' and only 6 teams will qualify to this round
