RULES

  • Event photographers are simply professional photographers armed with the skills and equipment to capture emotions, reactions and memories in high-pressure environments. They work in environments where they need to think on their feet to respond to changes in lighting, event agendas and the needs of the client.
  • To be able to participate, all the participants need to be at least 18 years old. Along with college ID is mandatory.
  • Participants are limited to one submission per person and the same photo cannot be submitted more than once.
  • Photo should be in JPEG+RAW.
  • No editing should be done.
  • Photos can be taken in color, black and white and the using of filters is not forbidden.
  • The idea should be the photographers idea. It shouldn't be guided by any professional photographer.
  • DSLR cameras are encouraged.
  • There will be no phone photography.
  • Tripod can be used. Depends upon the photographer
  • Photo submission should be on time. Late submission will not be encouraged.
  • Only 1 photo should be provided to the submission depending upon on topic.
  • Topic will be given on spot.
  • 1 person in a team.
