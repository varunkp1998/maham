RULES

  • Number of participants 2 in each team.
  • Entry fee per team is 300/-.
  • Participants must accompany college ID card.
  • Each team can present a model which belongs to either physical science or biological Science. The model can be working or static.
  • Any necessary material for the exhibition must be accompanied along with the model by the participants.
  • Prizes are awarded for the most innovative and creative model with good presentation. Judges decision is the final decision.
