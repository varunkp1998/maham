RULES

  • Listen to Me is a 1989 American drama film which revolves around a group of college students who are part of the debate team, embarking on a journey for triumph
  • Loosely based on this premise is the competition 'Listen to Me' where each team has to showcase their debating/public speaking skills, teamwork, vocabulary, imagination and spontaneity.
  • Each team should have 2 competitors.
  • This event has three rounds, 1. WAR 2. THE AUCTION 3. LISTEN TO ME Each team have to go through the first 2 rounds with good results to be part of the final round, the panel discussion.
  • Instructions of the first two rounds would be given on the spot.
  • At the end of the first round the top 12 teams on the basis of the points will qualify to the second round.
  • At the end of the second round 4 teams will qualify for the final round, the panel discussion.
  • The topic for the final round would be given 12 hours before the start of the final round
