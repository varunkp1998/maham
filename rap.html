RULES

  • Time limit for each participant is 3 Minutes.
  • Sound tracks should be submitted 2 days prior to the event.
  • Maximum of 3 entries from each college.
  • Participants will not be allowed to refer lyrics while singing.
  • The participants should bring their own musical instruments if required in a performance.
  • Choice of song is open to the participants, but if any slangs or derogatory languages are found, it leads to elimination.
